About Us

Who We Are

Forisa Abadijaya Sejahtera, operating under the brand name Forisa Impact, is a business advisory and consulting firm dedicated to helping organizations achieve sustainable growth through stronger people, better governance, effective risk management, and regulatory compliance.

Established in 2022, Forisa Impact specializes in Human Resources, Legal, Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC), providing practical solutions that help organizations improve performance, strengthen accountability, and build resilient business foundations.

We believe that sustainable success is not driven by talent alone. It requires the right combination of people, systems, leadership, and governance working together toward a shared purpose. Our approach combines strategic insight with practical implementation, ensuring that recommendations translate into measurable business outcomes.

Today, we partner with businesses, institutions, and leaders to strengthen organizational capability, manage risks proactively, improve compliance, and create long-term value for stakeholders.

At Forisa Impact, we are committed to delivering solutions that are practical, sustainable, and aligned with the unique needs of every organization we serve.

HR Transformation

Manufacturing Company Turnaround

Business Challenge

High turnover rate of 35% annually, unclear job descriptions, and no performance tracking system in place.

Our Approach

Conducted comprehensive HR audit, redesigned organizational structure, and implemented KPI-based performance system.

Implementation

3-month phased rollout including manager training, system integration, and employee communication program.

Outcome

Turnover reduced to 12%, productivity increased by 28%, and employee satisfaction scores improved significantly.

Key Takeaways

Clear structure and measurable targets create accountability. Implementation support is as critical as strategy design.

Governance & Compliance

SME Compliance Framework

Business Challenge

Growing SME facing regulatory scrutiny, missing documentation, and no formal governance structure.

Our Approach

Gap analysis of existing compliance posture, risk assessment, and governance framework design.

Implementation

Developed SOPs, compliance checklists, governance charter, and trained internal audit team.

Outcome

Zero compliance violations in subsequent audit, 40% reduction in operational risks, improved stakeholder confidence.

Key Takeaways

Proactive governance prevents costly reactive measures. Documentation discipline is a competitive advantage.
